## Required labels

- **[TEXT]** — directly supported by *Cilappatikāram*.
- **[CROSS]** — supported by another early Tamil work after verification.
- **[HISTORY]** — supported by responsible historical research.
- **[INTERPRETATION]** — controlled performance or psychological reconstruction.
- **[CAUTION]** — a modern assumption or unsafe simplification that must not be presented as fact.

## Binding safeguards

- Use adult performers for all principal roles involving marriage, sexuality, coercion or violence.
- Do not assign modern psychiatric diagnoses to literary characters.
- Do not use chastity to erase Kaṇṇaki's anger, bodily autonomy or political speech.
- Do not use Kōvalaṉ's earlier failures to justify his execution.
- Do not reduce Mādhavi's profession and artistic knowledge to sexual availability.
- Mark all invented private dialogue and psychology as interpretation during development.
