# Vañci Scene Architecture — Part 2B

## Scope

Scenes `SCN-VAN-016`–`SCN-VAN-020` cover departure ritual, march, alliances, northern conflict and acquisition of the Pattini stone.

## SCN-VAN-016 — Departure becomes public performance
- **Canto:** 26. Taking the Stone
- **Source:** HWT-0174, HWT-0175
- **Function:** Present kingship through ritual, poets, performers and public recognition.

## SCN-VAN-017 — The army crosses landscapes
- **Canto:** 26. Taking the Stone
- **Source:** HWT-0176, HWT-0177
- **Function:** Show military movement with ethical obligations toward communities encountered.

## SCN-VAN-018 — Allies and administration sustain the expedition
- **Canto:** 26. Taking the Stone
- **Source:** HWT-0178, HWT-0179
- **Function:** Reveal diplomacy, fleet preparation and written administration.

## SCN-VAN-019 — Northern coalition and battle
- **Canto:** 26. Taking the Stone
- **Source:** HWT-0180, HWT-0181
- **Function:** Depict organised warfare rather than individual combat spectacle.

## SCN-VAN-020 — The stone is obtained
- **Canto:** 26. Taking the Stone
- **Source:** HWT-0182
- **Function:** Complete the expedition’s stated objective while preserving its political complexity.
