# Vañci Scene Architecture — Part 2A

## Scope

Scenes `SCN-VAN-011`–`SCN-VAN-015` move from royal reflection to monument debate, northern vow, proclamation, mobilisation and departure preparation.

## SCN-VAN-011 — The ruler hears the burden of justice
- **Canto:** 25. Vision
- **Source:** HWT-0166
- **Characters:** Senguttuvan, court
- **Function:** Transform Kaṇṇaki’s story into a question of kingship.

## SCN-VAN-012 — Pattini becomes a royal responsibility
- **Canto:** 25. Vision
- **Source:** HWT-0167
- **Characters:** Iḷaṅkō Vēṇmāḷ, Senguttuvan
- **Function:** Establish that memorialisation begins through recognition, not conquest.

## SCN-VAN-013 — Stone, place and legitimacy are debated
- **Canto:** 25. Vision
- **Source:** HWT-0168
- **Characters:** Court specialists, king
- **Function:** Frame the monument as a cultural and ritual decision.

## SCN-VAN-014 — A memorial decision becomes an expedition
- **Canto:** 25. Vision
- **Source:** HWT-0169, HWT-0170
- **Characters:** Senguttuvan, ministers, proclamation system
- **Function:** Move from ethical resolution into state action.

## SCN-VAN-015 — The state mobilises
- **Canto:** 26. Taking the Stone
- **Source:** HWT-0171, HWT-0172, HWT-0173
- **Characters:** King, councils, army
- **Function:** Show administration, timing, provisioning and institutional capacity.
