# Madurai Scene Architecture — Part 2

## Scope

Scenes `SCN-MDU-011`–`SCN-MDU-020` cover grief, palace entry, evidence and collapse of royal certainty.

## SCN-MDU-011 — Kaṇṇaki sees the body
- Source: HWT-0108–HWT-0112
- Function: Convert private loss into public accusation.

## SCN-MDU-012 — The city hears the complaint
- Source: HWT-0113–HWT-0116
- Function: Move from grief to civic witness.

## SCN-MDU-013 — The gatekeeper carries the challenge
- Source: HWT-0117
- Function: Show institutional access.

## SCN-MDU-014 — The court receives Kaṇṇaki
- Source: HWT-0118–HWT-0122
- Function: Place truth before authority.

## SCN-MDU-015 — The anklet is broken
- Source: HWT-0123–HWT-0126
- Function: Material evidence defeats assumption.

## SCN-MDU-016 — The king understands
- Source: HWT-0127–HWT-0130
- Function: Royal self-recognition of injustice.

## SCN-MDU-017 — Queen and king collapse
- Source: HWT-0131–HWT-0133
- Function: Show personal and political consequence.

## SCN-MDU-018 — Kaṇṇaki leaves the court
- Source: HWT-0134–HWT-0137
- Function: Move judgement from court to city.

## SCN-MDU-019 — The city faces accusation
- Source: HWT-0138–HWT-0142
- Function: Prepare moral catastrophe.

## SCN-MDU-020 — Fire begins
- Source: HWT-0143–HWT-0146
- Function: Transition into destruction.
