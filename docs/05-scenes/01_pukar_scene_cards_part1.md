# Pukār Scene Architecture — Part 1

## SCN-PUK-001 — Moral contract
- **Canto:** Prologue
- **Source:** HWT-0001
- **Form:** MIXED_PERFORMANCE · LOCKED_SOURCE
- **Action:** Political failure, Pattini recognition, causation and the anklet are declared as the work’s governing design.
- **Function:** Give the audience a formal promise without revealing every later event.
- **Turn:** The anklet is established as more than ornament.
- **Performance:** Voice, inscription, ritual recitation or object-led montage rather than explanatory dialogue.
- **Production:** Anklet macro-detail; three-world image vocabulary; title design; music motif.
