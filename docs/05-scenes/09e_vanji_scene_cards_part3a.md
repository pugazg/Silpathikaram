# Vañci Scene Architecture — Part 3A

## Scope
Scenes `SCN-VAN-021`–`SCN-VAN-025` move the Pattini stone from conquest material to ethical memory.

## SCN-VAN-021 — Victory places weight upon defeated crowns
- Source: HWT-0183
- Lock: LOCKED_SOURCE
- Function: Show defeated rulers and the political cost attached to obtaining the stone.

## SCN-VAN-022 — The river consecrates the stone
- Source: HWT-0184
- Lock: LOCKED_SOURCE
- Function: Transform the stone from expedition object into Pattini ritual material through Ganges consecration.

## SCN-VAN-023 — Victory remembers bodies
- Source: HWT-0185, HWT-0186
- Lock: LOCKED_SOURCE
- Function: Connect ceremony and warrior remembrance; honour includes human cost.

## SCN-VAN-024 — Māṭalan reconnects the epic
- Source: HWT-0187
- Lock: LOCKED_SOURCE
- Function: Bring Pukār, Road and Madurai losses into the Vañci memory system.

## SCN-VAN-025 — Return begins with release
- Source: HWT-0188, HWT-0189
- Lock: LOCKED_SOURCE
- Function: Release captive rulers and begin the return through landscape and song.

## Interpretive limits
- Do not make conquest the origin of Kaṇṇaki's truth.
- Do not remove the human losses remembered by Māṭalan.
- Do not treat consecration as a simple royal ceremony.
