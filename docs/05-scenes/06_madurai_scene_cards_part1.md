# Madurai Scene Architecture — Part 1

## Scope

Scenes `SCN-MDU-001`–`SCN-MDU-010` move from organised capital to institutional failure.

## SCN-MDU-001 — The city receives them
- Source: HWT-0090–HWT-0093
- Function: Establish Madurai as a living capital before tragedy.
- Focus: markets, streets, sacred institutions, guards and commerce.

## SCN-MDU-002 — Kōvalaṉ enters the artisan world
- Source: HWT-0094–HWT-0096
- Function: Connect need for wealth with specialised urban economy.

## SCN-MDU-003 — The anklet becomes a transaction
- Source: HWT-0097
- Function: Transform private marriage wealth into survival capital.

## SCN-MDU-004 — The goldsmith recognises opportunity
- Source: HWT-0098–HWT-0100
- Function: Introduce deception inside a legitimate craft system.

## SCN-MDU-005 — Expertise becomes accusation
- Source: HWT-0101
- Function: Show how reputation creates authority.

## SCN-MDU-006 — Palace information chain begins
- Source: HWT-0102
- Function: Trace movement from artisan claim to royal power.

## SCN-MDU-007 — The order moves faster than evidence
- Source: HWT-0103
- Function: Establish institutional haste.

## SCN-MDU-008 — Kōvalaṉ outside the market
- Source: HWT-0104
- Function: Isolate the victim from the system that judged him.

## SCN-MDU-009 — The irreversible mistake
- Source: HWT-0105
- Function: Mark the point where correction becomes impossible.

## SCN-MDU-010 — News reaches protection
- Source: HWT-0106–HWT-0107
- Function: Shift the narrative from Kōvalaṉ to Kaṇṇaki.
