# Production Design Bible — Method

## Purpose

This layer converts source-linked scene architecture into visual, material and spatial decisions for research, screenplay development and production planning.

## Evidence hierarchy

1. Tamil source concordance (HWT IDs)
2. Historical world dossiers
3. Scene architecture
4. Archaeological and historical references
5. Creative interpretation

## Every design decision must identify

- Source anchor
- Historical confidence
- Visual interpretation
- Production implication
- Research questions

## Design principle

The three kāṇṭam worlds are not interchangeable fantasy spaces.

Pukār represents circulation, wealth, performance and maritime exchange.

Madurai represents administration, justice, hierarchy and the collapse of moral authority.

Vañci represents memory, ritual transformation and state institutionalisation.

The Road represents ecological transition and human vulnerability.

## Status labels

- LOCKED_SOURCE — directly required by text
- HISTORICAL_RESEARCH — supported by external evidence
- DEVELOPMENT — creative exploration
- CAUTION — avoid unsupported assumptions
