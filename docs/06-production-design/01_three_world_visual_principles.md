# Three Worlds Visual Principles

## Pukār — City of Circulation

Core idea: abundance in motion.

Visual systems:
- port and maritime exchange
- merchant houses
- performance spaces
- festival movement
- gold, textiles and artistic display

Avoid:
- treating Pukār as only a palace city
- reducing Mādhavi's world to entertainment

## Road — Landscape of Transformation

Core idea: movement changes people.

Visual systems:
- ecological transitions
- water scarcity and abundance
- forest communities
- ascetic routes
- temporary shelters

Avoid:
- empty fantasy wilderness

## Madurai — City of Justice and Failure

Core idea: organised power and its collapse.

Visual systems:
- gates
- markets
- artisan quarters
- court spaces
- royal authority

Avoid:
- making the catastrophe only supernatural

## Vañci — City of Memory

Core idea: transforming experience into institution.

Visual systems:
- hill ecology
- royal procession
- military logistics
- shrine construction
- ritual continuity

Avoid:
- replacing Kaṇṇaki's agency with royal ownership.
