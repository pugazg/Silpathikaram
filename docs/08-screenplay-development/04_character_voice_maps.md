# Character Voice Maps

## Purpose

Define screenplay voice while preserving the source-linked character systems.

## Kaṇṇaki

Voice movement:

- domestic precision
- practical observation
- compressed grief
- evidentiary accusation
- moral declaration

Rule:
Her strongest lines emerge from truth established through objects, memory and consequence.

## Kōvalaṉ

Voice movement:

- confidence
- aesthetic sensitivity
- uncertainty
- regret
- acceptance

Rule:
Avoid making him only an offender or only a victim.

## Mādhavi

Voice movement:

- artistic vocabulary
- coded expression
- emotional restraint
- written communication
- renunciant reflection

Rule:
Her performance language is part of her dialogue.

## Kauṇṭi

Voice movement:

- observation
- ethical questioning
- practical instruction
- protection

Rule:
Wisdom comes from discipline, not prediction.

## Senguttuvan

Voice movement:

- royal authority
- reflection
- accountability
- memorial responsibility

Rule:
He receives a truth already recognised by others.

## Court voices

Pāṇḍya court:
procedure, command and judgement.

Cēra court:
reflection, responsibility and memory.
