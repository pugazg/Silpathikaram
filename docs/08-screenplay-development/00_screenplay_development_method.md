# Screenplay Development Bible — Method

## Purpose

This layer converts the verified source, character, scene, production-design and cinematography archives into screenplay pages without collapsing source fact, interpretation and invention into one voice.

The screenplay must remain dramatically legible to a contemporary audience while preserving the epic's material, political, ecological, performative and sacred systems.

## Governing evidence order

1. Tamil-source concordance and HWT anchors.
2. Verified scene architecture.
3. Character and relationship dossiers.
4. Production-design, material-culture and prop continuity records.
5. Cinematography and sound rules.
6. Screenplay interpretation.
