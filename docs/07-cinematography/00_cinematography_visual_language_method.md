# Cinematography and Visual Language Bible — Method

## Purpose

This layer converts the source-linked scene architecture and production-design dossiers into rules for framing, movement, lens behaviour, light, colour, sound perspective and recurring visual motifs.

The aim is not to impose one decorative style on the epic. Camera and sound must change as social protection, bodily condition, political authority and public memory change.

## Evidence order

1. Tamil-source concordance and HWT anchors.
2. Verified scene architecture.
3. Character and relationship dossiers.
4. Production-design and material-culture dossiers.
5. Historical and technical research.
6. Cinematic interpretation.
